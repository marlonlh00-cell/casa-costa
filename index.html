Where the right people,

Casa Costa Social Club is Honduras' most distinguished event experience — private gatherings on the Caribbean coast of Puerto Cortés, curated for a select group of individuals who appreciate quality, style, and the art of a well-lived moment.

Every gathering gets announced right here. Register and you're in the running — spots are limited, so not everyone makes it in.

Vision
"To become Honduras' most distinguished event experience — where the right people, the right atmosphere, and the right energy come together to define what a night out should feel like."
Mission
"To curate exclusive, private events where every detail — from the music to the crowd — is intentionally designed for a select group of individuals who appreciate quality, style, and the art of a well-lived moment."
